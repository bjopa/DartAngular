Half is a game where the players take turns, sequentially aiming for designated targets (in green to the left). The current player throws 3 darts aiming for the target and adds the score to his previous sum. For example; 1 double and 1 single hit (and 1 miss) on 18 yields 54 points.

Every round a player fails, with all 3 darts, to hit the current target their sum is halved, rounded up.

The aim for round #6 is to get a sum of 41, and all 3 darts MUST hit a target, meaning you can't hit for example Triple 7, Single 20 and miss the third.

Max score is 1157 - Good Luck!
